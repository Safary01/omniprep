Installation

Up and running in minutes

1
Download the file for your platform
Click the download button above for Windows, macOS, or Linux. The file will save to your Downloads folder.
2
Extract the archive
Right-click the downloaded .zip or .rar file and select Extract All (Windows) or open with your archive tool. This creates an OmniPrep folder.
3
Open the app
Open the extracted folder and double-click OmniPrep.exe (Windows), OmniPrep.app (macOS), or OmniPrep (Linux).
4
Log in with your OmniPrep account
Use the same email and password from your ₦500 subscription. No new account needed. Don't have one? Register here.
5
Start training
You are ready. Open any module and begin your UTME preparation.
