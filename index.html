Frequently asked questions
Can I use OmniPrep on my phone? ▼

Yes. OmniPrep works on any device with a browser — desktop, laptop, tablet, or smartphone. We recommend desktop or laptop for the CBT Simulator since JAMB exams are taken on desktop computers.

How do I pay? I don't have a card. ▼

Paystack supports multiple payment methods — debit card, bank transfer, and USSD (dial a code on your phone). You do not need a card to pay.

When does my access expire? ▼

Your access lasts one full year from the date you pay. Since JAMB is an annual exam, this covers your entire preparation cycle. You can renew the following year for another ₦500.

Are the questions the same as the real JAMB? ▼

Our question bank is built to match the difficulty, style, and subject coverage of real UTME questions. We draw from past JAMB papers and educator-reviewed questions. The goal is 100% similarity in challenge level.

What subjects are covered? ▼

English Language is compulsory for all candidates. You choose 3 additional subjects from: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Economics, Government, Literature, and Geography — matching the real JAMB subject list.

Is my data safe? ▼

Yes. Your account and exam data are stored securely on Google Firebase. Your payment is handled entirely by Paystack — we never see your card or bank details. We do not sell or share your data with anyone.
